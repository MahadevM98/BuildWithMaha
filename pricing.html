Honest small print, in big print

What the prices don't hide.

Is ad spend included in the retainer?
No, and it shouldn't be with any agency. You pay Google and Meta directly from your own account. You see every penny, and if we ever part ways, the account and its history stay with you.
Is there a lock-in contract?
No long lock-in. The setup fee covers the build, the retainer covers the running. If it's not producing, the guarantee handles month one and you can walk after that.
Why is there a setup fee?
Because the first month is the heaviest: account structure, tracking, page builds, automation. Charging it honestly upfront beats hiding it inside an inflated retainer.
Which plan should I pick?
Don't pick one yet. Book the teardown, and I'll tell you which plan the maths actually supports. Sometimes the honest answer is Starter, and I'll say so.
