Honest small print, in big print

What the prices don't hide.

Is ad spend included in the retainer?
No, and it shouldn't be with any agency. You pay Google and Meta directly from your own account. You see every penny of spend, and if we ever part ways, the account and its history stay with you.
Is there a lock-in contract?
No long lock-in. The setup fee covers the build, the retainer covers the running. If it's not producing, the guarantee handles month one and you can walk after that. Systems that work keep clients without contracts forcing them to stay.
Why is there a setup fee?
Because the first month is the heaviest: account structure, tracking, page builds, automation. Charging it honestly upfront beats hiding it inside an inflated retainer. It also means everything built in that phase is paid for and owned by you.
Which plan should I pick?
Don't pick one yet. Book the 15-minute audit, and I'll tell you which plan the maths actually supports for your market and budget. Sometimes the honest answer is Starter, and I'll say so.
