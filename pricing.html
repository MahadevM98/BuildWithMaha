The small print, in big print
Is ad spend included in the retainer?
No, and it shouldn't be with any agency. You pay Google and Meta directly from your own account. You see every penny, and if we ever part ways the account and its history stay with you.
Is there a lock-in contract?
No long lock-in. The setup fee covers the build and the retainer covers the running. If month one doesn't produce, the guarantee handles it and you can walk after that.
Why is there a setup fee?
Because the first month is by far the heaviest. Charging for it honestly and upfront beats hiding it inside a padded retainer, which is the usual trick.
Which plan should I pick?
Don't pick yet. Book the teardown and I'll tell you which plan your market and budget actually support. Sometimes that answer is Starter.
