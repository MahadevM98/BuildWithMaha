The Three Leaks

Most wasted ad spend drains out of one of these three places. The guide shows you how to find yours and fix it, usually without spending another penny on traffic.

01

The Click

Your ad pulls in browsers and rate shoppers instead of people who are actually ready to move. Cheap clicks, wrong person.

02

The Page

A ready buyer lands on a page with fifteen things to do and no single reason to book. So they do nothing and leave.

03

The Follow-up

The enquiry comes in, then sits. Nobody reaches the person while they still care, and a warm lead goes cold.

Same ad spend. Often the same ads. A funnel that finally stops leaking on the way to your calendar. That is the whole guide.
