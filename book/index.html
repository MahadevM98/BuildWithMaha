Before you hesitate

The reasons people almost don't book.

Is this actually free, or a sales call in disguise?
Free, and not a disguise. I tear down your funnel and tell you the leaks. If it makes sense to work together I'll say so at the end, and if it doesn't I'll say that too. Either way you leave with the notes.
What do I need to have ready?
Just access to your current ads (Google or Meta) and the page they point to. If you've got an old account you're unsure about, bring it — that's often where the clearest leak is hiding.
I'm not sure my setup is "good enough" to show.
That's exactly the point of a teardown. The messier it is, the more there is to find. No judgement — the goal is to fix it, not grade it.
What happens after the 15 minutes?
You'll know your three leaks and the first fix to make. If you want to handle it yourself, the notes are yours. If you'd rather I build it, we talk about which plan fits. No pressure to decide on the call.
I'm busy. Is this worth fifteen minutes?
If you're spending money on ads that aren't turning into booked clients, fifteen minutes to find out why is the highest-return quarter-hour in your week. And it's capped at fifteen.
