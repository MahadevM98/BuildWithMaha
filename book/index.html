Straight about fit
Book if
  • You're a mortgage broker or adviser in the UK or US
  • You're spending on Google or Meta and enquiries don't match the spend
  • You want the truth about your funnel, not a sales pitch
Don't bother if
  • You want someone to celebrate impressions and clicks

The reasons people almost don't book
Is this actually free, or a sales call in disguise?
Free, and not a disguise. I tear down your funnel and tell you the leaks. If working together makes sense I'll say so at the end, and if it doesn't I'll say that too. Either way you leave with the notes.
What do I need to have ready?
Access to your current ads and the page they point to. If you've got an abandoned old account, bring that as well. It's often where the clearest leak is hiding.
My setup is a bit of a mess. Should I tidy it first?
Please don't. The mess is the point of a teardown. There's no judgement here, only fixes.
What happens after the 15 minutes?
You'll know your leaks and the first fix to make. Handle it yourself and keep the notes, or ask me which plan fits. There's no pressure to decide anything on the call.
Is this really worth fifteen minutes of my week?
If you're paying for ads that aren't becoming booked clients, finding out why is the best-paid quarter hour you'll have this month. And it's capped at fifteen. I respect the clock.
