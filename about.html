About

One person. One number that matters.

I'm Mahadev. I run BuildWithMaha as a one-person operation on purpose: the person who audits your funnel is the person who builds it, runs it, and answers when something needs fixing. No account managers in between, no juniors learning on your budget.

The background

Six-plus years in the trenches.

I've spent over six years in digital marketing, including agency work, before going independent. I've seen how agencies handle finance clients from the inside: the bloated reports, the click-counting, the setup that never gets revisited. BuildWithMaha exists to do the opposite of that.

The credentials

Certified, and current.

Certified by Google and Meta, with additional certification from Canonical. The certificates matter less than the habit behind them: this field changes monthly, and I keep up so my clients don't have to.

The focus

Mortgage brokers and advisers, deliberately.

I work with mortgage brokers and independent financial advisers in the UK and US. Specialising means I already know your buyer's search behaviour, your compliance constraints, and which offers actually get the phone ringing. A generalist learns that on your money.

The setup

Remote-first, response-fast.

I work remotely with clients across UK and US time zones, with overlap hours for calls and same-day responses as the norm. The lead system I sell is the lead system I run: enquiries to my own pipeline get answered in hours, not days.

How I work

Three rules I don't break.

No vanity metrics, ever.

You will never get a report celebrating impressions or clicks. The numbers I report are enquiries, booked calls, and what each one cost. If those aren't moving, I say so first.

You own everything.

Your ad accounts, your pages, your tracking, your data. Built in your name from day one. Agencies that hold accounts hostage are running a retention strategy, not a marketing one.

Honest answers beat comfortable ones.

If your budget is too small for a channel, I'll tell you instead of taking the retainer. If the cheapest fix is your page and not more spend, that's what I'll recommend. It costs me money sometimes. It's also why the guarantee is safe to offer.

The fastest way to know if we fit is fifteen minutes.

Bring your current numbers. I'll bring the leaks. If I'm not the right person to fix them, I'll tell you that too.
