A short file on meJune 2026

One person. One number that matters.

I'm Mahadev. BuildWithMaha is a one-person operation on purpose. The person who audits your funnel is the same person who builds it, runs it, and picks up when something looks wrong.

i.

The background

Six-plus years in digital marketing, agency-side and independent. I've seen how agencies handle finance clients from the inside, which is a large part of why this business is set up the way it is.

ii.

The credentials

Google and Meta certified, plus Canonical. The certificates matter less than the habit behind them: this field changes monthly, and keeping up is part of the job.

iii.

The focus

Mortgage brokers and IFAs in the UK and US, and only them. Specialising means I already know your buyer's search behaviour and your compliance constraints. A generalist learns that on your money.

Three rules I don't break
1.

No vanity metrics, ever.

You will never get a report celebrating impressions. I report enquiries, booked calls and what each one cost. If those aren't moving, you hear it from me first.

2.

You own everything.

Ad accounts, pages, tracking, data. Built in your name from day one. An agency that holds your accounts hostage is running a retention strategy, not a marketing one.

3.

Honest answers beat comfortable ones.

If your budget is too small for a channel, I'll say so instead of taking the retainer. This occasionally costs me money. It is also the only reason the 30-day guarantee is safe to offer.
