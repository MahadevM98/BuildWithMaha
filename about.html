About

One person. One number that matters.

I'm Mahadev. I run BuildWithMaha as a one-person operation on purpose: the person who audits your funnel is the person who builds it, runs it, and answers when something needs fixing. No account managers, no juniors learning on your budget.

The background

Six-plus years in the trenches.

Over six years in digital marketing, including agency work. I've seen how agencies handle finance clients from the inside: the bloated reports, the click-counting. BuildWithMaha does the opposite.

The credentials

Certified, and current.

Certified by Google and Meta, with additional certification from Canonical. The field changes monthly, and I keep up so my clients don't have to.

The focus

Brokers, deliberately.

I work with mortgage brokers and IFAs in the UK and US. Specialising means I already know your buyer's search behaviour, your compliance constraints, and which offers get the phone ringing.

How I work

Three rules I don't break.

01

No vanity metrics, ever.

You'll never get a report celebrating impressions or clicks. The numbers I report are enquiries, booked calls, and what each one cost. If those aren't moving, I say so first.

02

You own everything.

Your ad accounts, your pages, your tracking, your data. Built in your name from day one. Agencies that hold accounts hostage are running a retention strategy, not a marketing one.

03

Honest answers beat comfortable ones.

If your budget is too small for a channel, I'll tell you instead of taking the retainer. If the cheapest fix is your page and not more spend, that's what I'll recommend. It's why the guarantee is safe to offer.
